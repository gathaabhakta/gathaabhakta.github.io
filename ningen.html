Ningen

About

Ningen are cryptids assumed to live in the sub-antarctic region. The Ningen have been described as being humongous, “blubbery, whale-like creatures,” whose smooth, pale form vaguely resembles the head, torso and appendages of a human being. They are considered to be whales with human features.
I just find them so interesting and cute!
