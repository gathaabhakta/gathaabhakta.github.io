Tsuchinoko

About

The Tsuchinoko is a snake-like cryptid from mountainous regions of Western Japan. These creatures are commonly reported as slithering deep within the watery caves of Shikoku and Honshu. Signs a tsuchinoko may be in your area include hearing a mouse-like squeak coming from a river, or in some cases, a seemingly human voice mimicking your conversations from the depths of a dank cave.
